Epigenetics 101

What it is:

Gene regulation, not gene mutation. Your genes don't change, but how they're expressed does.

What it isn't:

Not fate. Epigenetic marks can be dynamic and tissue-specific.

Why it matters:

There are measurable links between your daily habits and your biology. What you do today can leave marks that affect your health tomorrow.

🧠 Brain & Stress
